Lauv releases music video for &quot;Paris in the Rain&quot;

May 03, 2024
blog

On 16th November, Lauv released the music video for his single “Paris in the Rain”. The singer has recently released another single, “A Different Way”, in collaboration with DJ Snake.

If you don’t know this rising star, we must tell you a bit about him. Lauv is an American Musician who garnered attention after he released his singles “I Like Me Better” and “The Other”. He is a notable songwriter who has co-written songs for artists like Cheat Codes and Demi Lovato.

Directed by Chase Smith, “Paris in the Rain” is a beautiful love track that talks about the significance of the beloved in a lover’s life. The presence and subsequent effect of love are compared to Paris as it looks in the rain. This slow track then celebrates the kind of love that does not need any embellishments for its fulfillment. If one listens to the song carefully, one gets the vibes of “I Like Me Better” in the single.
