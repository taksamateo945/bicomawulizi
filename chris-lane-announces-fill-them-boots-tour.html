Chris Lane Announces Fill Them Boots Tour! Celeb Secrets Country

May 16, 2024
blog

Chris Lane has some big plans to fill, as he announces the Fill Them Boots Tour for this fall!

After having wrapped the 25+ sold out show series for his Big, Big Plans Tour, Lane is hungry for more.The country music star will be serving up some of his fan-favorite hits and even more new music at 31 stops.

The tour will kick off October 28 in Memphis, TN and wrap March 12 in Pittsburgh, PA. Making some noise from coast to coast, Lane will be joined by incredible artists along the way.

Fans can anticipate performances from Tyler Rich at all show dates from October through December. ERNEST will then be passed the baton, as he will join the show for all January through March dates. Lastly, as if the Fill Them Boots Tour was not already full enough with incredible talents, Lily Rose will be performing at all dates from coast to coast.

Recently, Lane has been releasing fan-favorite track after track, such as “Summer Job Money,” “That’s What Mamas Are For” and more!

The smash hit song that titled this highly anticipated tour, “Fill Them Boots,” is skyrocketing up the country radio charts. With over 1.4 million views on YouTube alone, it’s not surprising to see why fans can not wait for this concert series.

To get your ticket for this knock-out tour be sure to mark your calendar for June 22! Pre-sale for tickets begins June 22 at 10am local time, with public on-sale beginning June 25 at 10am local time at ticketmaster.com

Check out the full list of tour dates below to see if this can’t-miss concert is coming to a city near you!
