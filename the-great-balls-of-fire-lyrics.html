The Great Balls Of Fire Lyrics

March 11, 2024
blog

Great Balls of Fire is a rock and roll song written by Otis Blackwell and Jack Hammer.

It was first recorded by Jerry Lee Lewis in 1957 and became one of his biggest hits, reaching number two on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and number one on the R&B chart.

The song’s upbeat tempo and Lewis’s electrifying piano playing and wild stage performances made it a classic of the rock and roll genre.
