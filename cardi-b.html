Cardi B is one of the most influential female rappers of all time, known for her distinct aggressive flow and outspoken lyrics. She gained fame through her videos on social media platforms and later joined the VH1 reality show Love & Hip Hop: New York as a cast member.

Career

Cardi B began her career as an exotic dancer and became popular on Vine and Instagram for her videos. In 2015, she became part of the Love & Hip Hop: New York show, where she shared her musical dreams and personal life. She quit the show after two seasons to pursue her music career.

In 2016, she dropped her first mixtape, Gangsta Bitch Music, Vol. 1, followed by a second volume in 2017. She landed a record deal with Atlantic Records and released her first single, Bodak Yellow, in 2018, which went viral. In 2018, she came out with her first studio album, Invasion of Privacy, which topped the Billboard 200 chart.

Personal life

Cardi B grew up in The Bronx with her Dominican dad and Trinidadian mom. She has a younger sister called Hennessy Carolina. She went to Renaissance High School for Musical Theater & Technology and later joined Borough of Manhattan Community College but dropped out.

Net worth

In 2023, Cardi B’s net worth was estimated at $80 million. She earned most of her income from music sales, streaming royalties, touring, endorsements and merchandise sales. She has also appeared in several commercials, films and TV shows.
