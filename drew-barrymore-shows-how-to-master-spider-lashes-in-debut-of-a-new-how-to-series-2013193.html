Drew Barrymore Shows How to Master &quot;Spider Lashes&quot; in Debut of a New How-To Series

July 15, 2024
blog

Who better than Flower Beauty founder and famed chameleon Drew Barrymore to teach makeup application? The actress and cosmetics mogul, who’s always been fearless about trying new beauty trends, is launching a new social media program called #TipTuesday.

Every Tuesday, starting today, the Big Miracle star and mom will post a video on FlowerBeauty.com answering a beauty question either Tweeted to @flowerbeauty (with the hashtag #TipTuesday) or posted on its Facebook wall. 

Us Weekly has the exclusive video, above, in which the new mom shows how to create “spider lashes.” In the tutorial, Barrymore, looking fresh and pretty with bright lipstick and rosy cheeks, reveals the secrets to mastering the look (hint: don’t be afraid to use your fingers!).
