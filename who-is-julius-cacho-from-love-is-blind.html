The cast of Love Is Blind Season 2 was just unveiled. Julius Cacho will be one of the new contenders in Season 2. The program will begin on February 11, 2022, with 30 fresh faces seeking to find love. The participants on Netflix’s reality program will go on blind dates. They will get to know one other’s personalities while separated by a wall that prevents them from seeing how the other person appears. The participants will only be able to see one other after they have mutual emotions for each other and desire to go forward with their relationship.

Who is Julius Cacho from Season 2 of ‘Love Is Blind’?

Julius Cacho, a logistics manager, is 39 years old and resides in Chicago. According to his Instagram bio:

“You’ll never make a move if you wait for the ideal moment.”

Cacho’s Instagram has been turned to private for the time being. In a YouTube promotional video for Love Is Blind, he remarked on his dating outlook:

“I’m most likely thrilled to get to know this individual and see whether we can genuinely have anything going together.”

He also mentioned that eating with one’s mouth open is his biggest pet hate. Julius Cacho may seem shy, but it will be intriguing to watch how he starts up talks with his dates on the program. Aside from him, the program has chosen other participants with distinct personalities who will shine once the show premieres.
