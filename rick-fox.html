Career

Rick Fox began his professional basketball career after the Boston Celtics selected him 24th overall in the first round of the 1991 NBA draft. As a member of the Celtics, he was named to the NBA All-Rookie Second Team in 1992. In the same year, he was released by the Celtics and signed with the Los Angeles Lakers. After the 2003–04 season, Fox was traded back to the Celtics, but he chose to retire instead. Fox also represented Canada on the international stage twice, once at the FIBA World Championships in 1990 and again in 1994.

Moving on, he starred in the 1994 film “Blue Chips” as a member of the Texas Western basketball team. He played Terry Hastings in the movie “Eddie” two years later. Fox then played the ladies’ man Chick Deagan in the 1998 film “He Got Game.” However, he landed the most crucial role of his early acting career in 1997. He played Jackson Vahue, a jail inmate, in HBO’s prison drama Oz.

Fox has also appeared in “To Your Health” episodes. He was in Resurrection, 1-800-Missing, the Crime Wave/Odd Ball episode of Nickelodeon’s The Fairly OddParents, and Street Time. Similarly, in the film Holes, Fox portrayed baseball player Clyde ‘Sweetfeet’ Livingston.

Rick Fox Wife, Marriage

Rick Fox is a divorcee who is currently single. Vanessa Williams, an actress and singer was his wife. After eloping in the Caribbean in the summer of 1999, they married again in September 1999 in New York City. Their daughter, Sasha, was born in May of 2000.

After images of Fox kissing and holding another woman were published in The National Enquirer in mid-2004, Fox’s representative announced that the marriage was “on the verge of divorce.” Fox divorced Williams a few months later, in August 2004. The split between Fox and Williams, on the other hand, was amicable enough for the two to reunite onscreen some years later on the television sitcom Ugly Betty.

Fox dated actress Eliza Dushku from October 2009 to June 2014. According to reports, Rick was also linked to Sharon Stone (2006-2007) and Kristin Davis (2007-2008). He’s also been married to Kari Hillsman, with whom he has a son named Kyle (born 1993).
